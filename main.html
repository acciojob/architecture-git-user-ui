Architecture Around the World

Modern Architecture

Modern architecture emphasizes clean lines, open floor plans, and the use of modern materials such as steel, concrete, and glass. It focuses on functionality and simplicity while maximizing natural light and space.

Historic Architecture

Historic architecture reflects cultural heritage and craftsmanship. Styles such as Gothic, Romanesque, and Renaissance architecture showcase intricate detailing, stone construction, and symbolic design elements.
